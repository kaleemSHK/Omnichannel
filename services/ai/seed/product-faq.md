# BlinkOne Home Fiber — Product FAQ

## Fiber 200 plan
Fiber 200 provides up to 200 Mbps download and 50 Mbps upload. Unlimited data applies to residential use in Oman. Standard installation is included for new customers in Muscat, Seeb, and Bausher when the building is on the green coverage list.

## Fiber 500 plan
Fiber 500 provides up to 500 Mbps download and 100 Mbps upload. Unlimited data. Free installation for new subscribers in covered zones. Upgrade from Fiber 200 requires an active contract with no outstanding balance; upgrades complete within 24 business hours.

## Fiber 1 Gbps plan
Fiber 1 Gbps is available in select towers and villas with XG-PON ONT support. Contact sales for a site survey. Business SLA options are sold separately.

## Wi-Fi router
Every home plan includes one Wi-Fi 6 router on loan. Additional mesh nodes can be rented for OMR 2 per month per node. Return the router within 14 days after service cancellation to avoid a OMR 35 equipment charge.

## Contract term
Residential contracts are 12 months. Early termination fee is OMR 50 if cancelled before month 6. No fee after month 6 with 30 days written notice.

## Support hours
Technical support is 24/7 by phone and WhatsApp. Billing desk hours are Sunday–Thursday 8:00–18:00 Oman time.
