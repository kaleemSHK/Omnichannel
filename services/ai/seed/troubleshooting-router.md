# Router & Connectivity Troubleshooting

## No internet — red LOS light
If the ONT shows red LOS, the fiber line may be cut or the ONT is not registered. Do not factory-reset the ONT. Escalate to field operations with the customer address and ONT serial number.

## Slow Wi-Fi only
If Ethernet speed test is good but Wi-Fi is slow, ask the customer to stand within 3 meters of the router for a test. Recommend 5 GHz SSID for laptops and TVs. Suggest mesh node if the home is larger than 120 m².

## Router reset procedure
Press the reset button for 10 seconds only when instructed by support. After reset, PPPoE credentials must be reprovisioned from BlinkOne NOC — customers cannot self-enter PPPoE on retail firmware.

## WhatsApp support scripts
Greeting: "Thank you for contacting BlinkOne support. Please share your registered mobile number or account ID."
Outage in area: "We are aware of a maintenance window in your area until [TIME]. No action is needed on your router."

## Speed test expectation
On Fiber 500, LAN speed test should show at least 400 Mbps download to a wired laptop. Wi-Fi results vary; below 150 Mbps on Wi-Fi alone is not a line fault.
