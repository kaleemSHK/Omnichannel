# BlinkOne Billing & Payments

## Invoice cycle
Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month for the previous month. Payment is due within 15 days. Late fee is OMR 5 after day 20.

## Payment methods
Customers may pay by bank transfer, card on the customer portal, or at authorized retail partners. Always quote the account number printed on the invoice header.

## Dispute process
To dispute a charge, open a ticket within 30 days of the invoice date. Provide the invoice number and line item. Billing reviews within 5 business days. Service is not suspended while a valid dispute is under review.

## Plan change billing
Upgrades are prorated from the change date. Downgrades take effect next billing cycle. Downgrade from Fiber 500 to Fiber 200 is allowed once per 12 months without fee.
